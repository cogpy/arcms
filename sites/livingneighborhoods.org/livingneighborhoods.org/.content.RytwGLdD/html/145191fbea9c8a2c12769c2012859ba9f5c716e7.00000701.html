COMMUNITY DISCUSSION

Encourage a spirit of community growing from the very first day, with community discussion

ONE-ON-ONE DISCUSSION WITH COLLEAGUES AND FUTURE NEIGHBORS As the generative code develops, you need to work hard to get at what people really have in their hearts. We have often found it best to use a slow process of one-on-one discussion with people who matter (partners and families and businesses, for instance) to reach their deepest intuitions. These discussions should then be milked for their most important content and combined with the stock of material which is being incorporated in the common generative code.
