WE HOPE THE MATERIALS ON THIS WEBSITE SUPPORT THE EFFORTS OF YOUR COMMUNITY TO BUILD, OR REBUILD, YOUR NEIGHBORHOOD. IF YOU WOULD LIKE MORE SUPPORT FROM US, PLEASE CONTACT US AND LET US KNOW THE WAYS IN WHICH YOU NEED HELP.
WRITE TO US We are very interested in learning about the projects you are working on, especially if you need advice to make the website work for you. Completing the worksheet on the Work Plan page will provide us with the information we need to be of assistance to you.
HELP AND SERVICES An overview of the types of help we offer, appears on the Services page. Possibilities range from low cost assistance, to building design, to full professional services for building large-scale or small neighborhoods.
REGISTER YOUR INTEREST We have a list of people who are currently receiving help from us, and who are trying, in their own way, to build innovative forms of neighborhood. We are also in touch with people who are themselves interested in this new way of thinking. We value all these people, and would be very glad if you would like to add your name, or your firm's name, to the list. If you provide your email address, we will notify you whenever significant new materials are added to this website.
