COMMUNITIES

HELPING A GROWING COMMUNITY TO ORGANIZE ITS SPACE AND ENCOURAGE DESIGN AND PLANNING BY THE MEMBERS
Many groups, and neighborhoods, and new communities, are trying to build a better world for themselves, paying more attention to the needs of individuals, to the needs of the community as a whole. Often these communities are already using A pattern Language to help stimulate their ideas.

On this web site we are offering new , powerful tools which will allow people to take charge in more significant ways than has been possible before -- and which allow people to create -- as they have always hoped -- the place of their dreams where they can live and work together.
