A VISION OF OUR CITY

EVOLVING PLAN AND PLANNING PROCESS

The process of our future, and what Duncanville will look like and feel like in the future, and what it offers to us in our daily lives, depends on:

  • Individual citizens knowing that they matter and their opinions can be really put on the table
  • The planning process being demystified
  • Realizing that the cutting edge process can be best implemented in towns the size of Duncanville.
  • Knowing that there is real potential to make Duncanville a beautiful place to live, work, and bring up children.
  • Knowing that there are practical ways that people can talk to the decision makers, and that the decisions-makers can talk to the people in the town.
  • The technology for a real community discussion and interaction exists now.
  • The technology for really checking out different alternative solutions exists now.

If you live in Duncanville, and want to take part, you can start here, and can make a contribution to the city, by giving your view on certain important and key questions about Duncanville

The quality of life and the future of Duncanville will originate, and continue, in the citizens' own answers to these questions.
