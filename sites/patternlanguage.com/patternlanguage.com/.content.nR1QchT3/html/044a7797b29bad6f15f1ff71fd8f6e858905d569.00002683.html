Genome Project for a Living World

The undertaking we are engaged in is vast.

We hope to create, and to stimulate the creation of generative sequences that will help people in all kinds of different endeavors, buildings, parks, roads, houses, hospitals, schools, communities and neighborhoods, will require the collective effort of many hundreds of people over the next two decades.

One conservative effort suggests that there may be as many as 40,000 sequences needed to generate a living world.
