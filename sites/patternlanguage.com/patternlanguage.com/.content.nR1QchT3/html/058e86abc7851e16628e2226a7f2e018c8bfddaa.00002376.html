DIAGNOSIS

  1. How healing works
    • At any moment in its history, some places in a city are vibrant, alive; others are decaying or dead. The way to help the city become better, is to pay attention, continuously, to these phenomena, just as a doctor does: To enhance and protect those areas which are in good shape, and to improve and repair those areas which are in bad shape. If we allow these two processes to guide our actions, then gradually, over a few years, the city will come to life.

  2. Participation in making these assessments
    • On the whole, people agree about which places have life and which do not. On this site, we provide an opportunity for people of Duncanville to influence the course of events, by identifying the places which are good, and those which are bad. The map people create together, changes constantly, in response to each new act: and continually drives the health giving development process of the future.
