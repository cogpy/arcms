CRAFTSMEN AND CRAFTSWOMEN

WE CAN HELP YOU FOCUS ON THE MAKING OF MORE BEAUTIFUL THINGS
AS PART OF THE ART OF BUILDING
It is not enough to make things with your hands, and to make beautiful things.

On this web site you will find attitudes and processes which can generate in you, the craftsman, ways of slowly unfolding a beautiful thing. Whether it is carving, casting, molding, inlaying, there is information on this site about new ways of doing these things so that they make a new kind of sense in today's world, and in our growing ultra-modern technology.

It is our aim to help you achieve the most highly sophisticated processes in a way which is efficient and consistent with modern construction technology, yet allows spirit to come forth in the made thing.
