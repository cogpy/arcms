HIGH DENSITY HOUSING IN INDIA

SEQUENCE 4: MAKING STREET SHAPE

The steps are:....

  • Give all streets and lanes their own width and shape
  • 1. Take segments of street, one at a time. A segment can be anything from 50 feet to 300 feet long
  • 2.  For each segment of street, give it a definite shape. This will impact on house lots and you will later modify the frontage line of each house lot to conform.
  • 3.  Make the ends of the street clear, with definite end-points.
  • 4.  Make a swelling in the middle
  • 5.  Locate the rough positions of front doors
  • 6.  Locate rough positions of stairs on individual lots, and make sure they are placed to help the street.
  • 7.  Make the street narrow unless there is a specially good reason for it to be wide
  • 8.  In narrow streets provide shade and comfort, with structures for shade
