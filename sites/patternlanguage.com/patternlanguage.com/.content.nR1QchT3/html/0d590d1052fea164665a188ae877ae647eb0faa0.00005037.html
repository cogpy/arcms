A Pure Case of a Sustainable Community

Fairview, Salem, Oregon, and the project for transforming Fairview, offers concepts, and solutions, which can be applied to many cities across the United States: and to many cases all over the world -- in Japan, Germany, England, France, China, SouthEast Asia, Africa.

The essential problems being tackled by our entirely new approach are:

  • User involvement for all residents and businesses
  • Formation of a coherent pedestrian oriented communityto demonstrate a new kind of development within our car-driven urban society.
  • Concern with the health of a growing ecological economy sustaining itself through its own effort
  • Absolute concern for the natural plants, water, wildlife in the community
  • A productive relationship with nature, agriculture, andhouses, workplace, and community.
  • Focus on human considerations, and a ground, throughout our efforts, on providing a place for the community which focuses the meaning of inner life.
