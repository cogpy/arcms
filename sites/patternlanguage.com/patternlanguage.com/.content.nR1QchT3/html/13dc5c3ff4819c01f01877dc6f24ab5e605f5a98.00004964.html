KEY ISSUES IN SHAPING A PUBLIC BUILDING

    A shape in harmony with the surroundings, and making positive space are what control the quality of all public buildings.
    Above all, the shape of the building must spring from the land, and buildings around, like a tree springing from a coppice -- it fits perfectly, from the moment of its inception.

    • The volume and the space around it are inspiring: they correspond to a vision of the whole which emerges, without artifice, and without ego, from what is there, and, as if in a dream, enhances these surroundings making them better than they were before.
