NOTE 1

Master the idea of three kinds of centers as described on the helpscreen. Remember:

There are natural, pre-existing centers in any room.

There are generic centers, like patterns, which come from our cultural expectations and hopes.

There are centers you will create in your design, and which will uniquely make your office work.

And, above all, remember:

ALL CENTERS ARE MADE OF SPACE.

In what follows everything you do has to do with focusing on these three kinds of centers, becoming aware of them, creating and shaping them to make your office practical.
