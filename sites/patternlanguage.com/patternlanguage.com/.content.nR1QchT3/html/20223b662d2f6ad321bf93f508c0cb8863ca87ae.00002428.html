VISION OF MAIN STREET

THE WONDERFUL PLACE TO GO
 
Scroll down our visionary plan for the future of Main Street and Duncanville

Imagine
  • Water, like a river, a series of elongated lakes, runs the length of Main Street.
  • The water is lined with trees, and with pedestrian walkways.
  • A chain of small pedestrian squares runs the length of Main Street growing in number each year. In a few years, the squares will be spaced out evenly, about two blocks apart, connected by the wide pedestrian thoroughfare with shady arcades and trees.
  • Each square has a personality: one for rollerbladers, one for toddlers, one with built in barbecue places, one for dancing, one for flea markets or farmer's markets.
  • Some of the squares are safe supervised places where children can play while you shop.
  • In the daytime, Main Street is alive with shops and business. At night, again, with cafes open, music, and promenade time.
  • Imagine a special trolley that goes up and down, so you don't have to walk with a tired child or grandma. Someday, the rail line is put back into use and you can come and go to the larger Dallas area.
  • The arterial main street with high volume traffic is maintained intact, on the far side of the water.
  • Parking lots, ample, and close to the space where people need to go, run all along the river.
  • Bridges cross, as needed, to give access.
  • Both sides of Main Street are wonderful with raised sidewalks on the opposite side of the water basins.
  • The place is full of life for working, strolling. loafing, playing, shopping or just hanging out.
