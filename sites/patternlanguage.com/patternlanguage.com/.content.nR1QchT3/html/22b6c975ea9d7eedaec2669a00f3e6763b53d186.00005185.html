EXPLANATION OF OUR PRACTICAL APPROACH

Our most fundamental idea is that a living world -- one in which land, plants, animals, and people are successfully co-adapted can only be created by the cooperative efforts of all the millions of us who inhabit the Earth: and that, in the long run, it will only come about if we share a conscious and deliberate purpose to achieve this goal together.

That means, involving literally all the millions of people on the Earth: a matter of centuries to accomplish. And it means that every act of construction or repair, no matter how large or how tiny, must be done with the purpose that it creates more life and is more harmoniously woven into the living fabric that exists.

It requires, above all, thought and purpose, about this harmonious life, in every single action that we take. If this website can stimulate an increased awareness of this cooperative activity, and can provide stimulus, and help, and support, for all those who wish to join in such a purpose, we shall have achieved our goals.

AN EVOLVING VIEW OF HOW -- GRADUALLY, IN FUTURE, AND IN PARTNERSHIP WITH MILLIONS -- WE HOPE TO STIMULATE AND VALIDATE, CONTINUOUSLY, THE SLOW AND CAREFUL REBUILDING OF OUR WHOLE LIVING WORLD
Uniqueness of each place -- the key to everything

turquoise background designates an active link

Implementation is the hardest part of our work. We have posted a good deal of material on theory and intent (inspiration). And we have now begun to post sequences -- the actual tools people need to shape their own environment (creation). But the implementation, in the long run, needs networks of people -- builders, architects, planners, local government, politicians -- all helping to permit and encourage this activity. It will take the longest, and it needs the most help from the largest number of people.

Implementation of the ideas expressed on this website, and the capacity to make the world genuinely alive with uniqueness of each part -- as it always existed in nature and as it also existed in traditional society -- requires far-reaching innovations in methods and attitudes for its success. This is a new challenge of the 21st century. It is our hope that efforts such as the effort on this website, will make it possible for the methods to be discussed, to spread, and to become effective, all over the world.

The methods require a new kind of integration of building and design. This follows because a set of drawings in the old-fashioned 20th-century form, cannot provide deep enough information, which is sensitive to variation in context, at every point. New forms of construction contract are needed; new forms of working drawings and specifications are needed;

Also required, is a wide ranging series of ways in which individuals and communities can take charge of their own environment. This includes ways of helping people do planing and design themselves, using the sequences; ways of helping them while they are doing this, through helplines, low-budget servces, and so on; ways of helping them put their conclusions (the rough designs) into the hands of builders who can implement what people want, without the elaborate and expensive present-day professional methods.

It includes, as a major component, encouraging architects and builders all over the world, to pariicpate not only in the design of buildings using these methods, but also in the creation, refinement and improvement of the sequences which people use to generate designs.

It includes finding ways in which suppliers of components and partially built custom components, materials, and so on on, make a change in their view so that they themselves cooperate in providing the kinds of low budget high quality unique components needed as part of the construction delivery system.

Also, these ideas are challenging enough, so that we believe people who seek to implement them, will need frequent access to one another, with opportunity for continuing discussion.

Perhaps the most important aspect of this living world, whether it is nature or human-made, is that every part is unique, because its form arises from a confluence of influences that are never twice the same. It is this which makes each spot, each corner, endlessly repeated, and repeating endlessly repeated elements -- yet memorable, and beautiful, and truly loved.
