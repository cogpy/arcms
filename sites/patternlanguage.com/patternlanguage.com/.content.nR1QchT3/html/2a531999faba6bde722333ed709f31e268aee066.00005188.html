Text Overview: What Is Living Structure?
......

   
Perhaps the most important aspect of this living world, whether it is part of nature or something human-made, is that every part is unique because its form arises from a confluence of influences that are never twice the same. It is this which makes each spot, each corner, memorable, and beautiful, and truly loved.

That goal, for Earth, is the target of our generative processes.

TEXT OVERVIEW

WHAT IS LIVING STRUCTURE?

In order to grasp the idea of living structure, and its relationship to generative methods, and the peculiar and special nature of the geometry which all living structure has -- whether in the realm of buildings, of organic nature, of inorganic nature, or in the realm of art -- a wide array of new theoretical concepts are needed.

We have presented these new theoretical ideas in a variety of ways, allowing a user of the site to browse, obtain books, to have access to archive material, and study discussion of these ideas with examples, tutorials, and in other formats.

Possibly most important of all is the emphasis on generative ideas and the theory of generative processes. The emergence of new theory, from the older pattern theory is also most instructive, are the widespread use of pattern theory as developed by Alexander now spreading throughout modern computer science and software engineering.

Essential books are The Nature of Order, Alexander's latest most comprehensive presentation of new theory, now coming out one volume at a time, starting July 2001, .The Timeless Way of Building, which first described the theory in an early form, thirty years ago, and the vast and widely read A Pattern Language, the first attempt made nearly thirty years ago, to provide a complete generative scheme for the built environment.

You may now see A Pattern Language and use it on-line on this site.

One of the most powerful ways to understand the meaning of what we are trying to do, is to look at pictures. We have pictures of buildings we have built and made which have these qualities in some degree; we have pictures of wonderful places made by other architects and builders today; we have pictures of buildings made in ancient times, in ways which also have these qualities.

It is only this which can make each spot, each corner, memorable, and beautiful, and truly loved.
