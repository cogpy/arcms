The work is based on the pioneering work of B.V.Doshi and his associates at Vastu Shilpa Foundation, and in particular on the pioneering project of Aranya which they built in Indore about 1989. That work was a limited success, but paved the way for great changes. In this work we hope to have corrected some weaknesses , set in place new and inspiring opportunities, and improved the approach to the problem, with a new set of tools developed over the last thirty years at the Center for Environmental Structure and PatternLanguage.com in Berkeley, California.

The dignity of the families is what matters most.
