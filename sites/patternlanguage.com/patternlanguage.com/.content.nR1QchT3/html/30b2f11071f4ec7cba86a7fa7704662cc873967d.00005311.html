HOW TO DO THE SIMPLEST THING


Bringing life into the environment is, ultimately, a matter of spirit. This is not religious mumbo jumbo, but a solid foundation, which embraces ecology, simplicity, economy of means, feeling, and common sense. It is a deep subject.

The four volumes of the nature of order in their entirety, deal with this subject. If you choose a certain kind of simplicity, you will find out that spirit is, in the end, only a matter of always doing the simplest thing.

However, this simple advice is not quite as simple as it seems. To grasp it, and to apply it, you must learn a new definition of simplicity, and then learn how to apply it.

But it ALWAYS boils down to this: Always do the simplest thing.
