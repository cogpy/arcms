Using the SVG Tool

The SVG tool is an online tool, available on this website, which permits you to design something by a series of steps, following very simple instructions. The tool is made so that even if you have no experience, a coherent well-drawn design will appear on the screen.

If you want to try this method, yourself, click here, to try your hand at designing a kitchen from scratch, or re-designing the kitchen you now have.

If you want to use this tool, and do not have an Adobe SVG viewer on your computer, click when prompted, and you can download the viewer to your machine in about two minutes.
