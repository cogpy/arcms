A NEW WAY OF DEALING WITH TOWN PLANNING:

THIS IS -- WE BELIEVE -- THE MOST VITAL ASPECT OF ARCHITECTURE IN THE 21ST CENTURY
The purpose of the discussions available on these pages is to help people in the movement to create an entirely different and more profound process by which we make our homes, our cities, and our lives.
We call the following material "discussions" because the more we understand about the problem, the more we realize that what is needed is a nationwide discussion about the way that town planning is going forward. It is a huge subject, troubling to many people, yet hardly touched in a profound and satisfactory way.
We call them "discussions", too, to reflect the fact that we are so ignorant, and that in a state of our current ignorance we need to come together, discussing the matter, until some new light begins to show itself.
