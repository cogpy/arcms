    Ultimately, we intend that the system available through this website will provide
  • (1) the means of securing land from local government
  • (2) A corps of trained engineers who will help
  • (3) Provision of financing from the world bank, to provide sites and services.
  • (4) Provision of teams of helpers for selection of families and training of families.
  • (5) Physical help, through engineering assistance, which will make it possible for thousands of families to succeed, on each site, in creating a world from their own dreams and desires, without intervention, or restriction, or unnecessary rules to narrow their vision.
  • (6) The money needed to get each new project started.
  • (7) The generative sequence which helps and allows each family to create their own space for themselves
  • (8) Help from local builders as needed to support their efforts
  • (9) Family access to computers and help from trained technicians so that the families can use these tools effectively

The fourteen sessions are:
....Community.... House lots.... Public gardens.... Street shape....House plinth.... House volume.... The breeze.... Main room.... Stairs.... Additional Rooms.... Rainwater.... Street Edge.... Doors and Windows.... Ornament
