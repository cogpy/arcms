A PLACE FOR FAMILIES
A TRUE CENTER WHERE PEOPLE WANT TO GO TO REFRESH, HAVE FUN, ENLARGE THEMSELVES

In ancient Greece, the Agora was a place for everyone to go. Philsophers went there to discuss and teach. The market was there, selling meat and fruit and vegetables. Entertainers were there, actors, jugglers, small booths with something interesting for children.

Food stands, games, entertainment, discussion had a more joyful, but also a more serious side to them, than our contemporary view of shopping centers or community centers -- both deadly in an almost socialistic sense, and largely devoid of juice or humor.

The essence of a place for families and community in Duncanville, will be a place which takes family life seriously, which is, for Duncanville, what our living room at home, is for us and our family. That is what we are aiming for.
