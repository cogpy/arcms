A letter from Ed Kuchtjak


From: Ed Kuchtjak
To: feedback@patternlanguage.com
Subject: A Possible Coffee Topic with Christopher or Discussion topic with a group?

I would like to hear some input from Christopher or others about Sarah Susanka's work with the "Not so Big House" concepts. She lists Christopher Alexander as an influence in both of her books. Having read her work, I agree she has been influenced by A Pattern Language. She seems to be garnering a fair amount of media attention in the mainstream press including "Oprah" "Newsweek" etc. As a devotee of Christopher Alexander's work, I've never let it bother me that his work seems to speak deeply to the faithful but remain largely unheard by the masses. How does the patternlanguage.com group perceive the public popularity to the Pattern Language Concepts as espoused by others such as Sarah Susanka? Any plans to join forces?

Thanks

Ed Kuchtjak
