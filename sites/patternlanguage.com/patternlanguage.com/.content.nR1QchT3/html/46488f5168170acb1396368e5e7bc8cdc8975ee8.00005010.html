THE SECRET OF OUR COMMUNITIES:
LIFE IS RELAXED, AND IT IS REAL. NO MORE PHONY ARCHITECTURE

Here is a recently finished clubhouse, in one of our communities. Paid by the members themselves, at their request and built by us.

An idyll: Boats, path, buildings, cars, people, hedges; all of it is real, all of it is under the control of the people themselves. It is ordinary life, we only help the real life to come to fruition, and we build the buildings for them, in the context of this way of thinking.
