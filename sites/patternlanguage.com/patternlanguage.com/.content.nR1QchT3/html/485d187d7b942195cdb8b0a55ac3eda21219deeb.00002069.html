LAYOUT OF YOUR PRIVATE OFFICE OR WORKSPACE

This is a sequence for laying out a comfortable office that allows one person to work effectively. It may be applied to a cubicle, or to a private office, or to an ordinary room. It is for a single workplace, standing alone or in a small group or in a large organization.

You will probably get the best results if you use this sequence after going through the Think-about-your-work sequence , and with the results you got from that sequence present in your mind as preparation for this one.

Watch a movie of an office layout which comes from the special needs of the person who is working there, combined with the impact of this sequence.

The office layout process has fourteen steps::
  1. Identify the natural centers of the room.
  2. Window place
  3. Placing your first center of activity
  4. Placing your second center of activity
  5. Placing your third center of activity
  6. Help on Positive Space
  7. Geometrical layout of the major centers.
  8. Secondary furnishings needed to complete the space.
  9. Help on Furniture
  10. Make Sparkling light
  11. Examine dead spaces
  12. More help on Positive Space. Is each space Positive?
  13. Getting the sizes right.
  14. Secondary furnishings needed to complete the space.
  15. Make, or buy, or order, the pieces of furniture to fit your design
