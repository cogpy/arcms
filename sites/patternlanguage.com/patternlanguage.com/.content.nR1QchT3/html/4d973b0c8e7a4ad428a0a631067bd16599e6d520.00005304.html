THIS WEBSITE AIMS TO

THIS WEBSITE AIMS TO

  • Give lay people WITH professionals information and tools to create buildings and neighborhoods for themselves -- to form a comfortable and humane environment
THIS WEBSITE AIMS TO

  • Empower users with user-friendly sequences that do not require sophisticated knowledge. It's a bit like making buildings the same way that origami instructions for folding a piece of paper into an airplane do not require an understanding of the complex spatial relationships inherent in the instructions
THIS WEBSITE AIMS TO

  • Develop the popular use of generative processes as means of communication between architects clients and builders, (instead of drawings, plans, designs and blueprints)
THIS WEBSITE AIMS TO

    Establish that
  • Generative processes are a powerful new way to bring forth complex structures that can self-correct and adapt
    • Generative processes are the only way to reach rich multi-functionality
      •
    • Generative processes, because they work through differentiation and division from an initial whole, are the only way to enhance deep unity
THIS WEBSITE AIMS TO

  • Develop support tools (drawing pads and models) that allow users to know what they are doing rather than working blindfold with undecipherable professional drawings whose ultimate result they cannot foresee
THIS WEBSITE AIMS TO

  • Create through more successful and satisfying building, sane spending, and the eliminating of costly mistakes, a better economic footing in the realm of design and construction
