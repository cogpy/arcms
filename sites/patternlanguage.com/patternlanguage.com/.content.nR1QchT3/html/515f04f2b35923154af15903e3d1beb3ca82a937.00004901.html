WHERE DOES BEAUTY OF FORM COME FROM?

The Key Issues

The way that a coherent form comes into a building, after the broad outline of the shape and plan has been sketched according to function, position, and need, is very interesting. Although it is, often, an inspired move -- it is also something that can be subjecte to a well-defined process.

This sequence aims, ultimately, to give you such a process

It can give you freedom, efficiency, effectiveness and productivity -- and is derived from your own preferences and a detailed examination of the way that structural order is introduced into a building, so as to prserve its social spaces.
