Ideas from other Cities

We are so used to the present development in America, so used to shopping malls, parking lots, food chains.... that we are almost unaware of what is possible, and of what kinds of thing have been done in other places, to make life friendly, fulfilling, worthwhile, in the city we live it.

To make sensible choices and decisions, it is helpful to start by summarizing some of the major kinds of things which can be done, but are not being done in "Edge City," today:

Here, for illustration, are some good places in contemporary urban settings today. Have a look at these pictures, and then, DOWN BELOW, vote your choices, as to which of these kinds of things you would like to see, happening somewhere, in downtown Duncanville, tomorrow
