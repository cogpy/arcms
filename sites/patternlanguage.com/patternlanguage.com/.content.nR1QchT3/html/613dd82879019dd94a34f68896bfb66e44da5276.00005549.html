IS THERE A NEW WAY OF THINKING IN THE AIR?

A way of understanding the world, its forms, its shapes, so that each thing has life -- to a greater or lesser degree. The shapes in a painting, the volumes and spaces in a sculpture, the space in a garden .. all have more life or less life.

What is on this web site is a way of thinking about the world which sees a painting or a carving in the same way as the whole world, and sees a city street in the same way as a tiny fragment of a painting.
