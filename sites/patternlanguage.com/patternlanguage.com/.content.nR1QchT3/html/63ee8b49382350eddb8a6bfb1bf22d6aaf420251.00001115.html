STEP 5: THE INNER CENTER OF THE TRANSITION

Now begin to imagine, inside the transition space, a center which is the place in the transition zone that you will most want to "be": this is the most important place.

It may be nearer to the street, or nearer to the house, or in the middle. It may be quite small, or it may occupy the entire space between street and door, but it must be a beautiful place where a person can BE.
