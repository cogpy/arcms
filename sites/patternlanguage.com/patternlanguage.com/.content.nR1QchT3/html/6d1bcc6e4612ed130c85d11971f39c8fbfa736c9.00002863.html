House Suite
THE VITAL TOOLS YOU NEED TO DESIGN AND BUILD YOUR OWN HOUSE

The following tools are still under development. They are not yet available on-line, but will be available in the next six months:
 
  PURCHASING LAND   If you are still shopping for land, this will give you a way of evaluating and comparing different sites, to get the truly important things essential to help make a beautiful home for you and your family.
 		  
 
  WHAT CAN YOU AFFORD   This will give you a way of establishing the cost of your house, how big it is, how much it will cost, and how you want to distribute the money. Costs are related to the region of the US where you live.
 		  
 
  CODE SEARCH   This will help you find out important code requirements such as setbacks, height requirements, and so on, that you will need to know in order to design your own house.
 		  
 
  HOUSE VOLUME LAYOUT TOOL   This shows you how to place the house in the best possible position on any given bit of land, including contours slopes, bushes and trees, in a way that you visualize
 		  
 
  DESIGN TOOL TO HELP YOU DESIGN THE HOUSE   This will give you a step by step process for laying out the house, in a way which you can manage even if you have no design experience
 		  
 
  FINDING AN ARCHITECT TO HELP   If you want we may be able to help you find an architect from our network, familiar with our way of work, in your neck of the woods. Or we can help you find an architect.
 		  
 
  FINDING A BUILDER TO HELP   We will do everything we can to find someone who will manage your project, and help you get it built. This may include full range project management services, to the occasional bit of advice.
 		  
 
  GETTING PERMIT DRAWINGS FROM YOUR SKETCH   Not too long from now we will have a system on line that can take a hand-drawn sketch, of the kind you have made by yourself, and will turn it into a simple permit drawing with which you may get a building permit.
 		  
 
  CHECKING YOUR SKETCH FOR CONSTRUCTION COST   Once you have a design sketch, this specialized cost estimating tool will allow to check and fine tune the cost of building your home.
 		  
 
  PREPARING A BUILDING CONTRACT   This will give you many options of different construction contracts we have developed, and will help you choose one and tailor it to your needs.
 		  
 
  SUB-CONTRACT FORMS   We have unique subcontract forms developed specifically to dovetail with the building contracts offered above.
&nsp;		  
 
  BUILDING DIRECTLY FROM YOUR OWN SKETCH   Our most advanced system, now in development, will allow a builder to build your house directly from the rudimentary sketch you make, will meet codes and permits, and will make a better house, all within a fixed, agreed-on budget.
 		  
 
  CONSULTING SERVICES FROM PATTERNLANGUAGE.COM   If you want even more help, we offer it. Ranging from support for the tools above, to full professional design and construction management services. We offer help, at whatever level you need it.
