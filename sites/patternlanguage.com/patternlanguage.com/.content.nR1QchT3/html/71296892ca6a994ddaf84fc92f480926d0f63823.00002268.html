Definition of a center

The evolution of a design in your mind's eye, through centers


....

The small-house design process has five main phases

  • Session 1: Garden position, House position, and Living room
  • Session 2: House volume character and the inside of the living room
  • Session 3: Walls, Entrance, Roof: the substance of the building
  • Session 4: Building structure
  • Session 5: Outdoor spaces, doors, windows and ornament

Each of these phases is long enough for a single session. You should complete a session, then have a rest, before you start another session.

Session 5: Outdoor spaces, doors, windows and ornament
  1. If you check all the outdoor areas around the house, are they all positive: entrance path, terrace, garden,.. is it true that each one is as positive and room-like as you can make it?

    Make every outdoor area around the house a positive center.
  2. Is the pattern of the windows beautiful from the inside of every room, and beautiful as a whole from the outside, and does every window have a pleasing pattern in itself?

    Make all windows and doors positive centers, and make each bit of wall between the windows a positive center, too.
  3. Does the house have a kind of ornament which makes you happy, and joyful, in a way that completes it and makes it a work of art?

    Make centers where they are missing -- whether color, shape, or carving, or line -- and call these ornament
