A Second Tutorial

To get a beginning feeling of what we mean by centers and positive space, and why they play such a decisive role in design and planning, please watch this movie. It shows the unfolding of the design of St. Marks's Square, over a period of 800 years. At each step you see that some center which is weak or missing, is made stronger -- and just by following this one simple rule, a beautiful and memorable thing is created gradually, step by step.
