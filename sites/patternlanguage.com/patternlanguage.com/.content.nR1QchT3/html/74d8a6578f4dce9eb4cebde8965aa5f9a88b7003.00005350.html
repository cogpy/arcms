The Solution  

  • Establish legal definitions which distinguish owner-occupier-users from absentee-developer-owners and corporations
  • Begin to distinguish between laws and processes that apply to owner-occupiers, and those which apply to absentee-owners and developers.
  • Create new user-friendly laws which apply to small scale developments in which user-occupiers are the principals.
  • Provide tools, models, and support which will enable user-occupiers to do what they want in a way which is excellent, and cheap and simple for them to do.

That, in brief, is the program of user-initiated development in Stafford County.
