NOTE 3

The state of mind in which you can arrive at an office that works well for you is low key and rather slow. This "easy" process requires a particular set of tools to get you there.

The carpenter's state of mind is humble.

If you follow this Sequence carefully, and think deeply about the space you will be working in, you may be rewarded with a space that can be comparably profound: not only adapted to your particular needs and created by your decisions, but also fitted to the deep spatial system that is latent in your office.

There is a chance that it may come to life.
