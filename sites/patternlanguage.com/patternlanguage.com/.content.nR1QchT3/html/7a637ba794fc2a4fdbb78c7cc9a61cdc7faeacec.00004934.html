REDEFINING OUR PROFESSIONS

NEW WAYS OF BEING AND WORKING IN THE WORLD OF BUILDING

It is our aim to help architects and builders work in new ways which are needed to create living buildings. We hope to support those who are aware of the need for new ways, and to provide the tools, services, mental pictures, financial advice, advice on methods of organizing new firms and works of construction.

In particular we seek to help both architects and builders provide project management to all the clients who believe that with the help of this web site, they can design, their own buildings, streets, gardens, for themselves, with the help of willing and skillful craftsmen, who seek the same goals; always to sustain the life which exists, and to support that life with modest designs that become great perhaps, by becoming almost invisible.
