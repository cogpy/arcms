PLACING A HOUSE VOLUME ON THE LAND:
WHAT'S WRONG WITH HOW IT'S DONE NOW.

When people place buildings nowadays, they often don't pay attention to the nature of the site at all. Even if they do, they may identify the best spot and then place the building there, thereby destroying that spot. Today's house makes no effort to shape and create beautiful places around itself.

This one fact about current practice -- banal and terrible siting for big buildings and small ones, has played a huge role in our pervasive urban ugliness. Change this one thing, and the world will be a better place!
