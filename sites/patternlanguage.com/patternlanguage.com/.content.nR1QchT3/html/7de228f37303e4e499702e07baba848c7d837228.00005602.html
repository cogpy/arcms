For membership in the Pattern Language Association please click here for the membership form

As a member of the Pattern Language Association, you will have access to the full library of materials available, including:

  • On line access to the 253 classic patterns with links between patterns
  • All sequences
  • Helpline services
  • Archive of unpublished papers, books, and pictures
  • Innovative contracts including new ways of building without working drawings but based on generative specification of construction process
  • Discussion possibilities with other architects who are using these techniques to make their houses better
  • So much more, we do more each day.

Further general information about the advantages of membership is on the membership form which comes up from this link.
