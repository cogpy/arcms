Our Vision The vision of the New Mexico Chapter of the American Planning Association is to be a catalyst for the innovative stewardship of local, regional and statewide resources in order to support healthy New Mexico communities.

This vision statement provides broad guidance for development and implementation of an action plan and informs every action the chapter takes or performs.
