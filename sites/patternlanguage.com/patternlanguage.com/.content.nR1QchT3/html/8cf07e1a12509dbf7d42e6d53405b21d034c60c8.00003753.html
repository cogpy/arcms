HOUSE LOTS       STEP 3: POSITIVE SPACE ON ALL SIDES OF THE HOUSE LOT

The space around the house lot, on all its open sides, must be positively shaped. This can only be achieved by a communal process in which families stand in each piece of public space that is being formed, imagine together how the space is best to be made useful, and then adjust their house lots edges to make the shape of the space.
