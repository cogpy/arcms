HOW TO THINK ABOUT YOUR WORK

Dream and think about your work to make it become more effective, more efficient, and part of a better life.

This is a sequence for preparing to make your work more effective.

It can give you freedom, efficiency, effectiveness and productivity -- and is derived from your own preferences and a detailed examination of how you do things when you are working at your best.

Watch a movie of someone using this sequence to think about their work.

This process for thinking about your work has eight steps::
  1. Mental preparation
  2. Understand centers
  3. Help on Centers
  4. Map your space
  5. Map existing centers
  6. List activities
  7. Help on Patterns
  8. Define personal patterns
  9. Prepare for physical design
  10. Dream about your ideal working conditions.
  11. Define the essence of the dream condition for each process.
