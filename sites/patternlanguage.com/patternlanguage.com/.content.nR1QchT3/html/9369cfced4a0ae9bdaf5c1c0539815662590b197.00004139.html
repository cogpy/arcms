We are very happy to include letters from passionate, or satisfied users.

There has been something of an outpouring of support and enthusiasm since we began. It is moving to those of us who created the company, and has a meaning that we feel must be shared. It would be pointless not to let all of you who share the passion we have, see the form in which others share this passion and enjoyment and express it.

So, here are a few of the letters we have received from you: your letters, with our thanks.

It is inspiring for us, and highly moving, and we thank you very much for sending us these letters and statements. They keep us going, and make us want -- for everybody's sake -- to do better.
