SEQUENCE OF STEPS FOR DESIGNING A FAMILY HOUSE

  4. PHASE 4: INDIVIDUAL ROOMS WITHIN THE MAJOR WINGS: BEDROOMS, DINING, KITCHEN, TV, RECREATION, FAMILY, OFFICE, RELAXATION, AND OUTDOOR SPACES
  5. PHASE 5: DOORS, WINDOWS, AND ORNAMENT
