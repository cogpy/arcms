KEY ISSUES IN PAINTING

    Making positive space is what controls all painting.
    If you watch the film of Matisse painting, you see how every act does these things:
  • It creates positive space, somewhere in the painting, often by using the possitite color from the one which forms the figures: the figures are created by drawing the space next to them
  • It is the positive space which does the most to help bring harmony into the picture and force, at the next step
  • The process always forms a local center
  • It always forms a large center in the picture and forms centers around that larger center to support it and make it fresh.
