HOME SWEET HOME

Are you one of the many people who come to this site because you are interested in your own home? You want to make your house more comfortable. You know that mistakes can be costly. You are unsure of how to go about remodeling.

This special welcome page gives a sample of some of the material available on the site which would help in remodeling projects. the welcome page is also so that new visitors can decide if membership in the patternlanguage association suits their needs.

On the Inspiration page
is a brief description of a pattern and a pattern language. We put, as an example, the pattern 159 about how light in a room can be beautiful or not. When you are thinking about your home environment, it is helpful to think out which patterns are important to you and how to link them together. It makes talking to a builder or contractor about what you want a lot easier.The now classic text which gives this site its name has 253 such patterns, available here on-line. In a similiar manner look at a brief description of a sequence .

scroll through the example of how to, step by step, make an important change. As an example, we've put in the link to thewindow placement sequence

On the Inspiration page
you can look at short essays which will not only help you have a more successful project but enjoy it so much more! Here are links to How Buildings Smile and Uniqueness as the key to all successful design.

On the Implementation page
you will see the possibility of a helpline and other kinds of services, if you need someone to talk to when you are designing for yourself.
