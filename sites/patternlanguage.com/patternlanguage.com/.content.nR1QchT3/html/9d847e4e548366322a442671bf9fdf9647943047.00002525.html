HOUSE FOR A FAMILY

YOUR HOME IS IMPORTANT AND YOU MAY FAIL TO GET THE MOST OUT OF YOUR PROCESS OF HOUSE DESIGN

Getting things in the right order--that controls the quality of the house design more than anything else.

Here are some of the aspects of the sequence which you will find on these pages:
  1. We start with a way of letting your mind's eye talk about your ideal setting. Thats not so easy to do and then translate into reality.

  2. We give a great deal of emphasis to the placing of the house in a harmonious way, on the land

  3. We also give great emphasis to the outdoors as a place where you live; it adds enormously to the useful space of a house.

  4. A critical issue is that you place the main rooms, in the order that will make the house coherent for you.

  5. We give great emphasis to the shape of the house, as a whole, and the beauty and restfulness of the overall form. That affects the way you feel every day, when you live there.

  6. The small spaces of a home,-- when added in in just the right order, and in the right amounts, make all the difference.
There is considerably more. However, even if you only get these six elements right, the house will be much, much, better
