HOUSE PLANS FOR STUDY

For years we have had requests from people who want to buy plans of houses we have designed and built. In response to these requests, we have decided to make plans of a few house designs from our archives available. These low-priced plans may be helpful for people who want to understand more about the nature of the houses that come from the processes we advocate, or for those with a limited design budget, who would like to modify and build and live in one of our houses. All the drawing packages contain scale plans and elevations, and many include full construction drawings. Please see each design for further details of what drawings are included.
