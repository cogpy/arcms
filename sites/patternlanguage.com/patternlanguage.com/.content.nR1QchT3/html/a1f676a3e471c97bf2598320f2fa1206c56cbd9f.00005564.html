MAYORS AND CITY MANAGERS

HOW YOUR CITY CAN SERVE THE NEEDS OF INDIVIDUALS

In the new era of the 21st century it becomes possible to address peoples needs, wishes, and dreams at a level which far surpasses what was possible in the 20th century.

Tools are available, on this web site, that can get people involved, effectively, in planning, streets, houses, public places, parks, greens, ecology, front yards, painting houses, taking care of community spirit. All these things will increase well-being, increase economic welfare, and -- above all -- increase emotional and spiritual well being of the people you care about.

Check out the tools, and find out how people in your city can use them effectively. It will cost the city almost nothing.
