SEQUENCE TWO       HOUSE LOTS

Outline: We shall now initiate a reciprocal process in which lots are chosen by families for themselves, and exterior space is shaped, piece by piece, lot with space and space with lot, positive and negative together, while each gets adapted by the shaping of the other.

This process is to be done by the families, helped and guided by the engineers

Go to step 1: FAMILIES CHOOSE AND ROUGHLY DEFINE THEIR HOUSE LOTS

....Rough positions.... Unequal size and shape.... Positive space on two sides.... Patched street space.... Small lanes.... Two sides on every lot....
