Here's the way it works. Once you submit the information given above, to register your group, your group receives its own webpage and order form. Each individual then visits the website, and pays for his or her own copy. When the target date is reached, the books are shipped, with additional savings in group shipment.

The price is reduced for each student who joins, so the more students are in the group, the lower the overall price. Initial discounted price will start at $60, and may go as low as $54, depending on number of students. Shipping costs and state tax (if applicable) are additional, and will be figured in the price.

The students vsiit the web page, once it is running, and each student pays with his/her own credit card. The final price charged is the price shown at the hour when the shipment closes.
