THE RULES OF THE GAME

You can put anything you want into your house design, at whatever size you say.

Each thing you put in costs you money.

Try to make a combination of things which makes the best house. The game will not allow you to go over $50,000. You keep adjusting quantities, until you have the best house you can figure out

Show yourself what is the most beautiful and practical house you can make for $50,000!!!
