GARDENERS

IN THE ATTITUDE FOUND ON THIS SITE, BUILDINGS AND GARDENS ARE ALWAYS SEEN AS PART OF A SINGLE WHOLE

In the recent past gardens and outdoors have been thought of as separate from the inside of the buildings they are part of.

We consider the plants, gardens, and living creatures part of the building, and p[art of its life. Pages on this web site do whatever possible to encourage you, as gardeners, and architects, and users, and builders, to respect this attitude of love for the living things which make the garden central to the living in the house.
