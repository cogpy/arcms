Positive Space

The pedestrian space, to be truly useful, and pleasant to the soul, must be "positive": that means, it is coherent, partially enclosed, it has shape, you feel as though you are in a room, almost. The pedestrian space should, ideally, be made of a series of linked spaces, each positive in this sense. Enclosure can be provided by buildings and building fronts, walls, trees, small buildings, hences, fences.

The developer will receive points, for the degree to which the pedestrian space in his project is all positive.
