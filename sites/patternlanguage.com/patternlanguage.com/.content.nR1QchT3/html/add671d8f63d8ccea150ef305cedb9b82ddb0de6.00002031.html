HOUSE FOR A FAMILY

This is a sequence for designing a house for a family.

It allows you to design your own house, according to your own wishes.

Your well-being, your sense of life, is what matters most.
These steps give you the tools to design a comfortable, economical house, that can make life beautiful.

Watch a movie of a house design unfolding

The house design process has these main steps
  • PHASE 1: The size and volume and position on the land, including the layout of the garden
  • PHASE 2: Placing the major elements inside the volume: The living room, the entrance, and the stair: and the major intermediate units which fill the volume and make up the organization of the house:
  • PHASE 3: Building structure: foundation, walls, entrance and roof
  • PHASE 4: Individual rooms within the major wings: bedrooms, dining, kitchen, TV, recreation, family, office, relaxation, and outdoor spaces,
  • PHASE 5: doors, windows and ornament

Each of these phases is long enough for a single session. You should complete a session, then have a rest, before you start another session.
