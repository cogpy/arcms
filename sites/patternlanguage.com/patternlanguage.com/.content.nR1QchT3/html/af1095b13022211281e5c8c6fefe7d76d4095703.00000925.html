THE CES ARCHIVE

For our members, we plan to provide access to the vast collection of unpublished papers and paintings and building plans by Christopher Alexander.

The archival papers, as they become available, will be in downloadable format. Some of the paintings will be made available as posters. Building plans will be made available in reduction copies.

Most of these items have never been seen by the general public.
