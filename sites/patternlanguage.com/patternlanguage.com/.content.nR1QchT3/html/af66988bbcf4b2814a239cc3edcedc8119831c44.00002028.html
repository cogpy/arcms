A HOMELESS SHELTER

This is a sequence for designing a place where homeless people can be well.

The fear of being homeless, the cold, the hunger, and the utter displacement and fear of being homeless in the universe not just on earth -- these are the guiding issues, which must be tackled head-on to provide a useful and humane solution.

The essence is that you must be design a place where you yourself would like to stay -- for a day, a night, a week, or longer. It ,muist go to your own sense of dignity, and help you to regain the sense of being a person.
The homeless shelter design process must be done with the homeless people themselves, since their dignity and empowerment can only come into existence of they are part of the process of making life better.
  • 1: Set garden position, House position, and Living room
  • 2: Define house volume and the inside of the living room
  • 3: Place walls, Entrance, Roof: the substance of the building
  • 4: Establish building structure
  • 5: Fix outdoor spaces, doors, windows and ornament
