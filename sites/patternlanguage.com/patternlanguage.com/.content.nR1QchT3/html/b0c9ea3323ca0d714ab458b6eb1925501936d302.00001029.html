DIFFERENTIATION

Unfolding is a step by step differentiation as in biology
Standard mechanical process

  • Design and budget constraints expressed (often poorly) by client
  • Blueprint (which is largely guesswork because nobody can actually know many things in advance) becomes legally binding
  • Bad guesses can not be changed without expense
  • Architect/builder/client are separate and sometimes at odds
Methodology of unfolding
  • Careful exploration with the client of what is really wanted
  • Work is done with large cardboard models that allow real simulation
  • Since each decision becomes the context for the next decision, each decision is handled as a separate step and concentrated on until is is right. It is the sequence of decisions which is the key to beautiful unfolding.
  • The budget ceiling is fixed but expenditures within it are adjusted according to project flow.
