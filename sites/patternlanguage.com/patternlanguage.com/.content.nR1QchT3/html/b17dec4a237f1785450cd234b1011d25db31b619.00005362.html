THE SECRET OF OUR MARCH TOWARDS DEVELOPER-FREE LAND IN STAFFORD COUNTY

A new form of human settlement
  • For several years, now, people in Stafford County have been working towards a new kind of land, which is not screwed up by developers, but belongs to people who live there and work there, instead! See history of our effort.
  • Urbanizations - cities -- were invented about 4000 years ago.
  • About 200 years ago we invented modern metropolis.
  • About 70 years ago we invented suburbs
  • Neither the modern metropolis nor the modern suburb really work as living environments.
  • Now we are starting to experiment with an entirely new form of living environment: a sustainable, interlaced web of open land, productive food-producing land, pedestrian streets, gardens, houses in small neighborhoods: we call it ECO-LAND.

This is what it looks like on people's faces.
  • The essence of ECO-LAND is a new kind of relationship between people and land. It is an interlocking relation of houses and natural land, which sustains, nourishes, works economically, supports people's needs, and supports the spirit.
