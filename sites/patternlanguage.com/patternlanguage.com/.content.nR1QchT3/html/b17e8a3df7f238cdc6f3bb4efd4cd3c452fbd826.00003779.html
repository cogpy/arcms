HIGH DENSITY HOUSING IN INDIA

SEQUENCE 3: MAKING PUBLIC GARDENS

The steps are:....

  • 1. Place public gardens with a total area 7-10% of the total community ground area
  • 2.  Choose roughly squarish spaces, where they fall among the house lots, among the houses, and at important nodes in streets. Define each one as a garden
  • 3.  Plant at least one tree in each garden. Protect the trees with a low wall, raised plinth so they are not destroyed while they are growing
  • 4.  Build one working fountain in every garden, and give the fountain fiscal priority, so it is funded, and maintained, before the houses themselves.
