In the end, the business that locate there will depend on decisions made by business owners, from the community and from outside. But if you express your feelings about it, and your wishes, and we then post the tally of your wishes ON THIS WEBSITE, where everyone can see it, it will send a powerful message to business people and would-be business people. There is a very strong likelihood, then, that you will GET your wishes.

Because, of course, the owners of those types of business you choose most often as your wished-for dream, will know they have customers in Duncanville, and will be more likely to have the courage to start a business there. Think it over. It is worth expressing your feelings.

You may stimulate people who have been trying to find the courage. Or you may encourage owners of a great business in some other part of Dallas to start a business on Main Street and make some money off you.Please check your five top choices and press submit
