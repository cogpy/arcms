WHAT IS VALUABLE IN DUNCANVILLE TODAY

What is remarkable, on Main Street of Duncanville today, is the charm, the truly small-town atmosphere, the grass, trees, the creeks.

Almost more valuable, are the small businesses, owneed and operated by people in Duncanville: Cox's market, the Green Nursery, the Learning Center.

These modest, informal places might seem negligible, if looked at from the point of view of contemporary ideas of development. But they are the places people have their heart attached to: they are the places people are fond of, they are the places which make Duncanville unique. They are above all, the places which a person remembers, when he thinks back to Duncanville.
