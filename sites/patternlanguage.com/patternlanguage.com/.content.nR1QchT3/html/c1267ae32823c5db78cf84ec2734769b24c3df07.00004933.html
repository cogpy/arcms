A letter from Kevin Little


From: Kevin Little
To: feedback@patternlanguage.com
Subject: thanks

I visited your site today; the Oakland bridge design pleased me and I wish you well in developing your project.

My training is in statistics and I describe myself as a specialist in information (particularly technical, numerical information...) but I have been inspired by Richard Saul Wurman's approach to information architecture...and I've had a copy of A PATTERN LANGUAGE on my shelf for several years.

In the pattern language catalog, I wonder whether there is a place for testing or learning patterns--in Nature, there are many interrelated feedback cycles at different levels of scale (time and space)...I contend that human developed designs should build in such feedback cycles (at different levels of scale--time and space) so that we can learn how to work and live more harmoniously with the rest of Nature. That's the focus of my business.

I look forward to visiting your site again, I've already posted it to several colleagues in the Wisconsin Green Building Alliance and friends.

best regards

Kevin Little, Ph.D.
