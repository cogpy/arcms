MEADOWS

This is a sequence for designing meadows in the buffer zones between city and wilderness.

It allows you to design and create meadows by pruning, tree care, cutting grass, and shaping positive space within the wilderness.

Watch a movie of a meadow unfolding

The meadow making process has these main steps
  • 1: Identify a naturally occuring center which can become a meadow
  • 2: Identify the natural boundaries of the meadow
  • 3: Prune underbrush and dead wood to make its open part more open
  • 4: Protect trees and bishes to form and intensify the natural boundary
  • 5: Embellish and protect individual centers within the meadow
