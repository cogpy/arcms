Patternlanguage.com is a community-based website dedicated to supporting all efforts to re-build the earth and care for it as a living structure.

Most web sites are financed by advertising. Patternlanguage.com is not. It is an association of members and sponsors. We finance the site through sponsorship, through the membership dues, and by our services.

Sponsorship

A sponsorship may be a one time gift, or a recurring annual gift. Every sponsor receives the privileges of membership. In addition some of our sponsors are advisors, and give us the benefit of discussion of ways in which we may improve and extend our services, and our effort to make the Earth a beautiful place, by relying on individual human effort.

All gifts received from sponsors are fully tax deductible, since the organization receiving the funds, PatternLanguage Foundation, is a 501(c)3, organization. If you would like to make a donation please indicate the amount, method of payment, and whether you would like the gift to be for a one year sponsorship, or a recurring gift.
