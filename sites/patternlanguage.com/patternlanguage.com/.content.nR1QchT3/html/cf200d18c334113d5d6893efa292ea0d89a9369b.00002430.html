ONE POSSIBILITY FOR MAKING A UNIQUE AND BELOVED ATTRACTING CENTER

If people are to be drawn from I 20 and I 67 there must be something to draw them. In discussions with the Chamber of Commerce, talks were about finding a Duncanville equivalent of the Eiffel Tower. Here is just one idea to get you thinking about different possibilities.
 

A CHAIN OF STEPPED LAKES Here we show a possible idea which can make Duncanville unique, and coherent, and potentially so well known so that everyone in Dallas will want to go there.

Our first exploratory idea of making Main Street truly unique and attractive for people all over greater Dallas, is to work with water and a series of small squares.
  • Imagine that there is a chain of small squares running the length of Main Street. Each is really small. They are spaced out evenly, perhaps on ever block, perhaps about two blocks apart
  • All in all, along the 3.5 mile length of Main Street, This means there might be as many as thirty of them -- though there will certainly be gaps.
  • Secondly, imagine water, a chain of stepped lakes, following the contours, and running for a good part of the length of Main Street.
  • And imagine this chain of lakes, lined with shade and trees, so that the pedestrian space, all along the water, is the place that people think of, and go to.
This particular solution may not be the right answer to creating a single visionary center that takes on the whole length of the street, and attracts visitors from all over Dallas and the southwest -- but something like it must be done.
