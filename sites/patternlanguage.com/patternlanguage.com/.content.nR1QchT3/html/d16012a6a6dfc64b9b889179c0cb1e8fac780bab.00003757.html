HIGH DENSITY HOUSING IN INDIA

SEQUENCE 2: PLACING AND MARKING HOUSE LOTS

The steps are:....

  • Preamble
  • 1.  Allow families to choose land, one by one, one at a time.
  • 2.  House lots are unequal in size and shape
  • 3.  Each lot is made to form positive public space jointly by families working together
  • 4.  Streets appear as patchwork of individual piecewise decisions by the families
  • 5.  Small lanes are put in when needed
  • 6.  Each house is seen as facing outward to each public space that it touches.
