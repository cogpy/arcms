GROWTH OF A

VILLAGE OR NEW NEIGHBORHOOD

This repeating seven-step sequence allows a community to build a vilage over time, applying the same rules repeatedly to the position of new lanes, position of new lots, and shape and position of new buildings

As a result of this repetition, the individual lanes, gardens and buildings will come to have their own appriopriate and unique character, according to the nature of slope, and trees, and social structure, and above all, the land.
The village growth process consists of the following sequence, applied over and over again
  • 1: What helps the neighborhood?
  • 2: Positive outdoor space
  • 3: Parking
  • 4: Building volumes to shape the outdoor space
  • 5: Another building volume
  • 6: Front door and entrance path
  • 7: Raise courtyard over parking
  • 8: Modify building volumes
  • 6: Front door and entrance path
