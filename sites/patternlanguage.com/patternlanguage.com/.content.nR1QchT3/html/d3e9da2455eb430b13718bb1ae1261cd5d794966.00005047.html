In search of a new planning process which will make Fairview a beautiful place, excellent to live in, and preserving and developing its beauties and amenities, the community has dedicated itself to a new kind of team work in the production of houses, thus:

THE TEAM: EVOLVING PLAN AND PLANNING PROCESS

An evolutionary process, able to meet the dynamic needs of a modern urban community, and based on five-way partnership between

  1. SHAREHOLDERS OF SUSTAINABLE FAIRVIEW ASSOCIATION  including both elected officers and administrative professionals of the company, led by the vision of Russ Beaton and Sam Hall
  2. CJR DEVELOPMENT  A shareholder's development corporation, dedicated to creating humane, ecologically sensitive houses,
  3. RESIDENTS AND FUTURE RESIDENTS OF FAIRVIEW  including all its involved citizens, families, and householders,
  4. PATTERNLANGUAGE.COM   will provide Christopher Alexander's urban planning expertise and access to new procedures for housebuilding in certain limited segments of the Fairview site, together with the medium of this website that will provide a vehicle of communication which supports our partnership,
  5. IN FURTHER PARTNERSHIP WITH  James Meyer of Opsis, Patrick Condon of the University of British Columbia, Steve Coyle of Lennertz Coyle, Portland, Stuart Cowan of Ecotrust, Lynn Peterson (transportation), 2020 Engineering, Gary Lawrence, and Katy Langstaff of Sustainable Systems Design.
