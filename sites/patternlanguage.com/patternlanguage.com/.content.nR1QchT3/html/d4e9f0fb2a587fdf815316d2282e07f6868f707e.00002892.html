WALK THE LAND

Start by visiting the land. Walk the land. Identify the place on the land which draws you, which is most beautiful to you, which you are most able to love...

In very preliminary fashion you might also begin to imagine where you would have to place the house so that the land is healed, and the one most important spot which is most beautiful to you is made better. How can you build the house so that it enhances and protects the beauty of the land?
