SUMMARY OF SEQUENCE

This is a sequence for placing a build on a piece of land.
The primary purpose is to make the building be in harmony with the land.

This is perhaps the most important thing there is in planning and building. At one time in the Earth's history almost everyone knew how to do this.

GENERATIVE SEQUENCE
Today, almost no one knows how to do it, and this is where the horrible destruction of mass development, chiefly comes from. Yet when done right, it makes all the difference to a city.

One approach which works, and which we have used on our projects for years, goes roughly like this:

  • PHASE 1: Walk the land
  • PHASE 2: Get a Topo of the land
  • PHASE 3: Diagnosis
  • PHASE 4: Build a model of the land
  • PHASE 5: Fix your favorite spot
  • PHASE 6: Roughly fix the position of the house
  • PHASE 7: Volume Studies on the model
  • PHASE 8: The harmony of the volume in the land is the criterion
  • PHASE 9: Finalize the volume, and make positive space everywhere
  • PHASE 10: Extreme simplicity

Each of these phases is long enough for a single session. You should complete a session, then have a rest, before you start another session.
