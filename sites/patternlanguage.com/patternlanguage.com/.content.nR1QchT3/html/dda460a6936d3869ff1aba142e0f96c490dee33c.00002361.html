DUNCANVILLE PEOPLE AND THEIR TOWN

MAIN STREET AS A PLACE TO GO
 
 

Like any community, a town needs a heart: a place at the center. Main street can become that place. People need an experience not yet available to them: a coherent, connected, environment for intense city activities: working, offices, stores, shopping, slow lanes and fast lanes, driving, cruising, parking, cafes, lunching, loafing, meeting, strolling. All ages, young and old, school and office, work-time, leisure time.

Above all this place needs so much character, that people want to go there just because of its beauty, its fascination, its character.

What is this new heart for the city to be? What will make it work? What will identify it? Whatever it is to be, we believe it is clear that Main street must stand out vividly from surrounding areas in greater Dallas. When we get it right, it will stand out to create a place people really want to go, a unique and significant center of urban and community life in Duncanville. Here we show a few very roughly expressed ideas which might become visions that can make Duncanville unique, and coherent, so that everyone will want to go there, and so that the economic engine will turn, and then take off.
