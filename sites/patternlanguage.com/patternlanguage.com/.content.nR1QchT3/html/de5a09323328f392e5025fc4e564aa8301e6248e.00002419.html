In search of a new planning process which will make Duncanville thrive commercially, a beautiful place, excellent to live in, and preserving and developing its beauties and amenities, the city has dedicated itself to a new kind of team work, thus:

THE TEAM: EVOLVING PLAN AND PLANNING PROCESS

An evolutionary process, able to meet the dynamic needs of a modern urban community, and based on three way partnership between

  • THE PEOPLE OF DUNCANVILLE  including as many of its involved citizens as wish to take part, and with a dedication to make a town which nourishes their own feelings, visions and desires,
  • THE CITY OF DUNCANVILLE  including Glenn Repp, the mayor and city council, Kent Cagle the city manager, Earle Jones, the director of economic development, Julia Jobe, president of the planning and zoning board, and city staff in all departments,
  • PATTERNLANGUAGE.COM   headed by Christopher Alexander and Jenny Quillien who will provide architectural and urban planning expertise and access to new procedures through the medium of this dedicated Duncanville website that updates daily, to provide the vehicle of communication which allows this three-way partnership to succeed.
