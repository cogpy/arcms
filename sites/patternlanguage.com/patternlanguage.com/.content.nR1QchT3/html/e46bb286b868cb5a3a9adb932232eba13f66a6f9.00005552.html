CITY PLANNERS

A NEW REVITALIZATION OF OUR CITIES
We hope that dynamic tools of citizen involvement on a block by block basis will provide for a new era in city planning and city reconstruction.

On this web site we shall provide tools for downtown areas, and for suburban neighborhoods, in a way that, we hope, will allow city planners and city officials to enter in a new way into effective participatory decision-making with the inhabitants, residents and business-people of cities.

Our aim is to help city planners generate a profound and supportive urban fabric in cities all over the world.
