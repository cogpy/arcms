COLOR. . . MORE COLOR. . OF COURSE COLOR. . .MANY COLORS

 

 

 

 

All melodies the echoes of that voice

All colors a suffusion from that light"

Samuel Taylor Coleridge 

 

This website is dedicated to the built

environment and building a better one.

When we build, be it house, city or

garden wall, we are making shapes

and spaces. Shapes and spaces are

geometry. Geometry and color interact.

This building from Tibet is a good

example of geometry and color joining hands.

 

If you want to have wonderful color,

you have to have a good geometric

structure.
