PEDESTRIAN STREET

This is a sequence for transforming or designing a pedestrian street.

It provides a community with the main steps needed to make a pedestrian street lively and a place people will want to "inhabit."

Watch a movie of a pedestrian street unfolding cycle by cycle

The design process has these main steps
  • Step 1: Overall configuration of the streets
  • Step 2: Crucial position of parking
  • Step 3: The central destination
  • Step 4: Views up side alleys and streets
  • Step 5: Positive space
  • Step 6. Rooms in the street
  • Step 7. Obstructed views
  • Step 8. Fun, games, theater
  • Step 9. Small centers along the way
  • Step 10. Street shape
