PLANNING PROCESSES

We are beginning to make the following models of planning available for cities and counties and communities. All of them are process-based, and all of them include methods of participation that allow people in the city or community or county to express their views, and to have positive effect on the ongoing development of the environment.

It is our aim to make models of this kind available, and to develop them, for any communities who are interested.
