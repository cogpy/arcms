HIGH DENSITY HOUSING IN INDIA

SEQUENCE 1: CREATING A COMMUNITY

The steps are:....

  • 1.  Preamble
  • 1.  Define the boundary of the community
  • 2.  Identify major points of connection to area outside
  • 3.  Identify important views
  • 4.  Identify special places in the land which have some soul and should be kept as important public spaces
  • 5.  Make marks at these points.
  • 6.  Establish a central chowk or wide public street which connects the most important existing centers
  • 7.  Show rough lines of how other streets feed into this chowk to make a possible, rough whole
