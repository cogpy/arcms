In doing this kitchen sequence we'll be asking you to be thinking and looking in ways that are different from the usual way of laying out or remodeling a room. Typically the appliances dominate the kitchen layout. The sink is usually placed under the main window. This layout process focuses on the activities and placing eating and working centers according to their importance. It is a way of thinking about how we construct a built environment around us that is deeply comfortable and meaningful. A kitchen is one small example of how buildings affect our lives and it fits into a larger cloth like a thread among many threads. For more on the philosophy behind sequences see philosophy
