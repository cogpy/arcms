BUILDERS NETWORK AND OTHER MEMBERSHIPS

REQUEST FOR MORE INFORMATION
It is our intention to build a network of cooperating builders and professionals all over the United States, and ultimately all over the world: a network of people dedicated to helping people create the built world, for themselves, in a fashion which adheres to the ideals embodied in this web site. We intend that this network will include builders, contractors, architects, planners, landscape architects, artists, artisans, crafts people of all kinds, ecologists, naturalists, developers and engineers. If you are interested in getting information about our proposals for this network, please e mail us, and we will send you information. Please include a fax number or a mailing address.
