The most basic idea: Every decision taken benefits both the private individual, and the public good. The processes steer people towards the coupling of these two perspectives on every single decision. In more detail:
  • We have clear goals expressed for making the whole better.
  • Fairview people have agreed on these goals
  • Every new project has to make something new and positive happen according to these goals.
  • Every penny that is spent has to contribute
  • Every project is judged, and given permission, according to the contribution it makes to the private place, and also to the immediate environment of the larger whole
  • Families will be scrambling to be part of it, once they understand that they they will become wealthy of pocket and of spirit by contributing towards the public good.
  • Every project whether it is a $1,000 project or a $100,000 project -- has the obligation to contribute.
  • Making that precise, and practical, is the beauty of the scheme!
