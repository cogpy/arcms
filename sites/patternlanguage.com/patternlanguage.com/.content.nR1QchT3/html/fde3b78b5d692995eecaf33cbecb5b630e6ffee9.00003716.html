This is a fourteen part system for creating housing in India. It allows people, regardless of income level and education, to make their own houses for themselves, according to their own wishes.

The dignity of the user is what matters most.

These fourteen sequences are intended to create a new way of approaching the daily life of urban Indian families, which allows each family the dignity of their own uniqueness, and gives them the tools with which they can achieve it for themselves.

INDIAN HIGH DENSITY HOUSING

The fourteen sessions are:
....Community.... House lots.... Public gardens....Street shape.... House plinth.... House volume.... The breeze.... Main room.... Stairs.... Additional Rooms.... Rainwater....Street Edge....Doors and Windows.... Ornament
