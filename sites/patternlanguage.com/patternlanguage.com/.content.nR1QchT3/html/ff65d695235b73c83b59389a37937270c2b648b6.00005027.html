INVITATION

The organization undertaking this highly complex venture is the Sustainable Fairview Associates, LLC., led by Russ Beaton and Sam Hall. The corporation started life two years ago, then, in 2001 bought Fairview Mental Hospital, the old Oregon State Hospital. It has 275 acres, southwest of Salem, and includes many old buildings, and much beautiful land.
SFA intend to build the most advanced, fully sustainable, economic-ecological community in the United States, with some 1200 dwellings, farms, light industry, and with at least half of the land left as nature or in agricultural production.
