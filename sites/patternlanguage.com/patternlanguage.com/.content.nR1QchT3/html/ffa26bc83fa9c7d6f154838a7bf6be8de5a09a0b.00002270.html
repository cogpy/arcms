Definition of a center

The evolution of a design in your mind's eye, through centers


....

The window design process has six main phases

  • Initial step: Look at examples
  • Session 1: Preliminary layout of most important window
  • Session 2: Choose subdivision according to the rectangle
  • Session 3: Strengthen centers in the subdivision
  • Session 4: Look at several windows, and choose simplest and most pleasing when repeated
  • Session 5: Make all minor windows lesser versions with less differentiation
  • Session 6: Check that every major window is a being

Each of these phases is long enough for a single session. You should complete a session, then have a rest, before you start another session.
